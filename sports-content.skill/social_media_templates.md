# Social Media Templates

These templates are optimized for the NASHAT CSR initiative. They are structured for high engagement, readability, and immediate impact.

---

## 1. Twitter / X (The Hook & Punch Format)
**Goal:** High virality, concise facts, quick engagement.
**Character Limit:** 280 (keep it under 240 for retrievability).

**Arabic Template:**
`[Emoji Hook]` `[Powerful Statement / Question]`
👇
`[Core Value or Fact - max 2 lines]`
💡 `[Actionable Tip or Insight]`

🔗 `[Link if applicable]`
`[1-2 Branded Hashtags]` `[1 Trending Hashtag]`

**English Template:**
`[Emoji Hook]` `[Bold Claim / Catchy Question]`
👇
`[Main Point / Statistic - concise]`
💡 `[Takeaway or Call to Action]`

🔗 `[Link]`
`[1-2 Branded Hashtags]` `[1 Trending Hashtag]`
