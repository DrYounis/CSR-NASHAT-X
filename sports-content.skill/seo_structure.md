# SEO Article Structure & Saudi Arabic Keyword Bank

## 1. Article Structure (H1, H2, H3 Guidelines)
When generating long-form content or blog posts, strictly adhere to the following SEO structure.

*   **H1 (The Title):** Must contain the primary keyword. Limit to 60 characters for best CTR.
    *   *Example:* كيف تبدأ ممارسة الرياضة في حائل؟ (رؤية 2030)
*   **H2 (Main Sections):** Clear, descriptive, and containing secondary keywords.
    *   *Example:* فوائد النشاط البدني في المجتمع السعودي
*   **H3 (Sub-sections):** Used for listing methods, steps, or breaking down complex ideas.
    *   *Example:* أفضل 3 حدائق للمشي في مدينة حائل
*   **Meta Description:** Keep under 155 characters. Start with a hook, include the primary keyword, and end with a CTA.

## 3. URL Slug Guidelines
- Use clear English translations or transliterations.
- Separate words with hyphens `-`.
- Keep it short.
- *Good:* `hail-community-sports-initiatives`
- *Bad:* `how-to-start-playing-sports-in-hail-region-during-ramadan-2025`
